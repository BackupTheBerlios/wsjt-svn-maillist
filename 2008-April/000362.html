Dear Colleagues,

Recently you will have noticed much activity in a new branch 
of the WSJT source-code repository.  This activity surrounds 
creation of a new program WSPR (pronounced "whisper"), 
designed for experimental low-power transmissions for 
propagation studies on the HF bands.  Some links providing 
additional details on WSPR have been placed on the WSJT home 
page, http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/ .

I am writing now to let you know that there will likely be 
further activity in the repository over the next week or so, 
as the code for WSPR matures.  If you wish to compile WSPR 
on your own, for the present I recommend using revision 674 
("Version 0.5"), which is stable and known to work well 
under Windows and Linux.  The program's evolution will now 
be moving toward more effective use of multi-threading, and 
as a consequence code at the "HEAD" of the repository is 
likely to be unstable for a few days.

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
